Goal: Client will label 100 common items expressively with 90% accuracy across 3 consecutive sessions.
Baseline: Currently labels 45 items with 80% accuracy.
Mastery Criteria: 90% accuracy across 3 consecutive sessions.
Measurement: Cold probe data, randomized presentation.

Goal: Client will answer "what" questions about present items with 80% accuracy across 3 consecutive sessions.

Mastery Criteria: 80% accuracy across 3 consecutive sessions in natural contexts.
Measurement: Language sample analysis, minimum 50 utterances.
